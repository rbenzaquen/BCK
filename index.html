Investment Model

The Fund seeks to mirror the investment outcomes of a cryptocurrency index, continuously monitored by our experts. Employing an active investment strategy, the Fund not only capitalizes on asset price fluctuations but also extracts additional returns through the implementation of DeFi strategies.

Investment Approach

Designed for a market characterized by intrinsic volatility and high risk, the Fund focuses on the most relevant and well-known assets. It aims to maximize profitability beyond mere price variations while maintaining liquidity to seize specific opportunities as they arise.

Fund Composition

The portfolio primarily invests in cryptocurrencies with significant market capitalization, predominantly Bitcoin (BTC) and Ether (ETH), the latter being staked to generate additional returns. It also includes stablecoins invested at favorable rates to optimize performance. A small portion of the portfolio is allocated to short-term opportunities in other identified crypto assets.
